About Us

Nari is more than just an app — it is a lifeline for women in distress. We provide a holistic approach to safety that empowers every woman to feel secure and supported.

Thoughts like "WHY ME?" haunts a woman for life and we are here to provide relief and safeguard the lady when she is in danger. Our mission is to create a world where women can move freely without fear.
